What SIPS is, in one sentence

Anti-na® SIPS is a dissolvable dietary supplement combining 2,000mg therapeutic ginger root extract and 1,000mg Bioenergy Ribose® (D-Ribose), formulated for sublingual absorption to address chemotherapy-induced nausea and fatigue (CINV) — manufactured in an FDA-registered facility in Irvine, California and tested for WADA banned substances.

The dual-action formula

Two ingredients. One mechanism.

2,000mg
Therapeutic ginger root extract

Acts as a 5-HT3 antagonist and NK1 modulator — the same receptor pathway targeted by prescription antiemetics like ondansetron, but via natural gingerols. Supports gastric motility, reducing the "heavy" feeling associated with CINV.

Ryan et al., 2012 — ASCO clinical trial
1,000mg
Bioenergy Ribose® (D-Ribose)

D-Ribose is the rate-limiting substrate in the pentose phosphate pathway for ATP synthesis. Chemotherapy agents place significant mitochondrial stress on cells — D-Ribose supplies the raw material for cellular energy recovery.

Teitelbaum et al., 2006
